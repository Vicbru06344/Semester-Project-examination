About Rootrise

We are a non-profit organization, founded in 2020 by Jessica Harris. Based in United Kingdom, is our core team. Our core team is joined by a few teams around the world, to help our cause.
Deforestation is our main concern. We are trying to plant as many trees as possible, as Deforestation is a real threat worldwide. We work with local communities and schools to bring awareness to our cause.
